Listen&Guess

  • To start the game press "Start game" button
  • The game consists of 20 words
  • Listen to the word
  • Choose your answer from an answer option list
  • If the asnwer is correct you'll get the choosen answer highlighted and underlined
  • If the asnwer is incorrect the choosen answer will get a line-through effect
  • Click the "Next" button to procced to the next question
  • If you don't know the answer press "Dont know" button to proceed to the next question
  • If you press "Dont know" button the right answer will be automatically highlighted and underlined
